TweakManager

By Unicorn808

Enable or disable your tweaks in bulk.

Description

TweakManager lets you create Tweakages (Tweak-Packages) that are lists of tweaks you want to disable or enable.You can copy, edit, and delete them, and of course enable them to disable your unelected tweaks. In the future you may even be able to share them!
