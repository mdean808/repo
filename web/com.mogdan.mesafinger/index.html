MesaFinger

By Unicorn808

Flip off anyone who fails FaceID or TouchID.

Description

This tweak shows the middle finger when TouchID or FaceID fails, so flip off all those snooping society snoopers, or yourself when your finger is wet. You can adjust the font size, message, color, and position.
